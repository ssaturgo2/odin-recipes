Breakfast Burrito

A fast DIY breakfast for the road that's made in minutes with scrambled egg, salsa, and cheese wrapped up in a tortilla.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tbsp salsa
  • 1 slice reduced-fat American cheese
  • 1 tortilla

Instructions

  1. gather Ingredients
  2. crack eggs into the bows
  3. add salsa, and stir
  4. microwave on high for 1 minute, stir, and cook for another minute or until firm
  5. place cheese in center of tortilla and top with egg mixture
  6. wrap it all up like a burrito
