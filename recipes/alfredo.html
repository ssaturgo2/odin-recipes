Chicken Alfredo

This easy Chicken Alfredo recipe includes golden pan-fried chicken breasts and tender noodles, coated in the most dreamy cream sauce ever!

Ingredients

  • 16 oz dry fettuccine pasta
  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast
  • 1 tsp italian seasoning
  • 3/4 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 3/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 3/4 tsp italian seasoning
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 2 cups freshly grated parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. make the noodels
  2. make the chicken
  3. warm the olive oil over medium-high heat
  4. Transfer the chicken to cutting board
  5. make the alfredo sauce
  6. add in the minced garlic
  7. bring to a gently simmer
  8. stir in the parmesan
  9. assemble
  10. divide the pasta among serving bowls
