Miso Chicken Noodle Soup

This miso chicken noodle soup, with thick, chewy udon noodles and a savory miso broth, is a tasty—and very easy—remake of traditional chicken noodle soup.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 8.5 oz frozen udon noodles
  • 4 oz rotisserie chicken, shredded
  • 1 tbsp white miso paste
  • everything bagel seasoning, to taste

Instructions

  1. In a saucepan over medium high heat, heat water to boiling
  2. Add udon noodles, cook until noodles are softened
  3. add rotisserie chicken. Turn heat to low
  4. remove 2 to 3 tbsp water, and stir miso paste into water
  5. mix diluted miso pasxtse into noodles
  6. cook for 2 to 3 minutes
  7. add bagel seasoning to taste
